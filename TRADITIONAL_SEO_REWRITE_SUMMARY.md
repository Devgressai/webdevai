## üîë KEY STRATEGIC DECISIONS

### **1. Positioning: Foundation, Not Competitor**

**Decision:** Position Traditional SEO as the **required foundation** that enables AI SEO and AEO to succeed.

**Rationale:**
- Avoids competition between service pages
- Clarifies the service hierarchy
- Encourages bundled service purchases
- Educates clients on why they need all three

**Messaging:**
> "Traditional SEO is not replaced by AI SEO or Answer Engine Optimization‚Äîit enables them."

---

### **2. Clear Scope Definition**

**Decision:** Define Traditional SEO through **three core pillars** with specific components.

**The Three Pillars:**
1. **Technical SEO** ‚Äî Crawlability, speed, mobile, indexing
2. **On-Page SEO** ‚Äî Content, keywords, structure, internal linking
3. **Off-Page SEO** ‚Äî Backlinks, authority, reputation

**Rationale:**
- Eliminates ambiguity about what "Traditional SEO" includes
- Makes the service tangible and understandable
- Provides clear buckets for service offerings
- Prevents scope creep into AI or AEO territory

---

### **3. Relationship Clarity**

**Decision:** Explicitly explain how Traditional SEO, AI SEO, and AEO work together.

**The Model:**
```
Traditional SEO = Infrastructure Layer
    ‚Üì
AI SEO = Acceleration & Intelligence Layer
    ‚Üì
AEO = Featured Snippet Capture Layer
    ‚Üì
Complete Search Visibility Strategy
```

**Rationale:**
- Clients understand why they need multiple services
- Each service has a distinct, non-overlapping role
- Positions Webvello as offering complete solutions
- Encourages upsells and bundled packages

---

### **4. AI Citability Optimization**

**Decision:** Structure content for easy extraction by AI systems and answer engines.

**Techniques Used:**
- Direct answer summary at the top (2-3 sentences)
- Question-based H2 headings in FAQ section
- Clear, concise answers (150-200 words per FAQ)
- Structured data (FAQPage schema) for all questions
- Bulleted lists for scannability
- Definition-style content formatting

**Expected Outcome:**
- Increased likelihood of being cited by AI chatbots
- Featured snippet captures for FAQ questions
- Enhanced visibility in Google's AI Overviews
- Better performance in voice search
