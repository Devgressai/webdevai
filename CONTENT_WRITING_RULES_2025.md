## üìã **Core Requirements**

### Word Count Minimums
- **Blog Posts:** 2,800 words minimum
- **Service Pages:** 4,000 words minimum
- **Quality over quantity** - every word must add value

### Writing Style (Jasper.ai Approach)
- **Conversational yet professional** - Write like talking to a smart friend

## üéØ **Content Structure**

### Blog Post Structure (2,800+ words)

#### 1. **Introduction (200-300 words)**
- Hook with a question, stat, or bold statement
- Identify the reader's pain point
- Promise what they'll learn
- Include primary keyword naturally

#### 2. **Main Content (2,200-2,400 words)**
Organize into 5-7 major sections with H2 headers:

**Section Pattern:**
- **H2:** Main topic (include keyword variations)
- **Opening paragraph:** Why this matters
- **3-5 subsections (H3):** Specific tactics/insights
- **Examples/case studies:** Real-world applications
- **Actionable tips:** What reader can do today

**Content Elements to Include:**
- ‚úÖ Statistics and data points
- ‚úÖ Expert quotes or insights
- ‚úÖ Step-by-step processes
- ‚úÖ Common mistakes to avoid
- ‚úÖ Best practices
- ‚úÖ Industry trends
- ‚úÖ Tools and resources
- ‚úÖ Real examples

#### 3. **Conclusion (200-300 words)**
- Summarize key takeaways (3-5 bullet points)
- Reinforce main benefit
- Strong CTA (call-to-action)
- Link to related services

#### 4. **FAQ Section (Optional but recommended)**
- 3-5 common questions
- Brief, direct answers
- Include long-tail keywords

---

### Service Page Structure (4,000+ words)

#### 1. **Hero Section (150-200 words)**
- Compelling headline with primary keyword
- Subheadline explaining unique value
- Primary CTA button
- Trust signals (years, clients, results)

#### 2. **Problem/Solution (300-400 words)**
- Identify 3-5 major pain points
- Explain how your service solves each
- Use "Before vs. After" framework

#### 3. **Service Overview (400-500 words)**
- What exactly you do
- Your unique approach
- Why it works
- What makes you different

#### 4. **Detailed Service Breakdown (800-1,000 words)**
Break into 4-6 H2 sections covering:
- Process/methodology
- Key features
- Technologies/tools used
- Timeline expectations
- Deliverables

#### 5. **Benefits Section (400-500 words)**
- 6-8 specific benefits
- Each with 2-3 sentences explaining
- Focus on outcomes, not features
- Use numbers and metrics where possible

#### 6. **Industry/Location Specific (500-600 words)**
- Local market insights
- Industry-specific challenges
- How you address local/industry needs
- Success stories from similar clients

#### 7. **Process/How It Works (400-500 words)**
- Step-by-step breakdown
- What client can expect
- Timeline and milestones
- Communication process

#### 8. **Case Studies/Results (300-400 words)**
- 2-3 brief success stories
- Specific metrics and results
- Client testimonials
- Before/after scenarios

#### 9. **Pricing/Investment (200-300 words)**
- Transparent pricing approach
- What's included
- ROI explanation
- Flexible options

#### 10. **FAQ Section (300-400 words)**
- 5-8 common questions
- Detailed answers
- Address objections

#### 11. **Final CTA (150-200 words)**
- Strong closing statement
- Multiple CTA options
- Contact information
- Next steps

## ‚úçÔ∏è **Jasper.ai Writing Style Guidelines**

### Tone & Voice
- **Confident but not arrogant** - "We're experts" not "We're the best ever"
- **Helpful and educational** - Teach, don't just sell
- **Empathetic** - Understand their struggles
- **Optimistic** - Focus on possibilities and growth
- **Direct** - Get to the point, avoid fluff

### Sentence Structure
```
‚úÖ GOOD: "Your website should load in under 3 seconds. Here's why."
‚ùå BAD: "It is important to note that website loading times should ideally be under 3 seconds."

‚úÖ GOOD: "Most businesses waste $5,000 on bad SEO. You don't have to."
‚ùå BAD: "Many businesses experience financial losses due to ineffective SEO strategies."
```

### Paragraph Guidelines
- **2-4 sentences per paragraph** (online readability)
- **One main idea per paragraph**
- **Use transition words:** However, Meanwhile, In fact, Here's the thing
- **Vary paragraph length** - Mix short punchy ones with longer explanatory ones

### Power Words to Use
- **Action words:** Transform, Accelerate, Dominate, Skyrocket, Unlock
- **Benefit words:** Proven, Guaranteed, Effortless, Simple, Fast
- **Emotional words:** Frustrated, Excited, Confident, Overwhelmed, Empowered
- **Authority words:** Expert, Professional, Certified, Specialized, Advanced

### Opening Hooks (First Sentence Examples)
```
"Here's a stat that'll blow your mind: [statistic]"
"Let me guess: [common pain point]"
"What if I told you [bold claim]?"
"Most [industry] businesses make this mistake: [mistake]"
"You're leaving money on the table. Here's how."
```

## üé® **Formatting Best Practices**

### Headers
- **H1:** Page title only (include primary keyword)
- **H2:** Major sections (include keyword variations)
- **H3:** Subsections (use long-tail keywords)
- **H4:** Minor points (rarely needed)

### Lists & Bullets
- Use bullet points for 3+ related items
- Use numbered lists for sequential steps
- Keep list items parallel in structure
- Add brief explanations after each item

### Visual Elements (Describe in Content)
- Call out where images/graphics would go
- Describe charts, graphs, diagrams
- Reference infographics
- Mention video content opportunities

### CTAs (Calls-to-Action)
**Primary CTA (Use 2-3 times):**
- "Get Your Free SEO Audit"
- "Schedule a Strategy Call"
- "Start Your Project Today"

**Secondary CTA (Use 1-2 times):**
- "Learn More About [Service]"
- "Download Our Guide"
- "See Our Portfolio"

## üîç **SEO Integration**

### Keyword Usage
- **Primary keyword:** 8-12 times naturally throughout
- **In H1:** Once (exact match)
- **In first 100 words:** Once
- **In H2s:** 2-3 times (variations)
- **In conclusion:** Once
- **Secondary keywords:** 4-6 times each
- **Long-tail keywords:** Sprinkle throughout

### Internal Linking
- **3-5 internal links per 1,000 words**
- Link to related services
- Link to relevant blog posts
- Link to location pages
- Use descriptive anchor text

### External Linking
- **1-2 authoritative external links per 1,000 words**
- Link to industry studies
- Link to statistics sources
- Link to tools/resources
- Always open in new tab

### Meta Elements
- **Title Tag:** 50-60 characters, include primary keyword
- **Meta Description:** 150-160 characters, compelling, include keyword
- **URL Slug:** Short, keyword-rich, hyphenated

## üìù **Writing Process**

### Step 1: Research (15 minutes)
- Analyze keyword intent
- Review competitor content
- Gather statistics and data
- Identify unique angles

### Step 2: Outline (10 minutes)
- Create header structure
- Plan main points for each section
- Identify where to place keywords
- Plan internal/external links

### Step 3: Write (60-90 minutes)
- Write conversationally
- Focus on value and insights
- Include examples and data
- Maintain natural flow

### Step 4: Optimize (15 minutes)
- Check keyword placement
- Add internal links
- Verify word count
- Review readability

### Step 5: Polish (10 minutes)
- Read aloud for flow
- Check for repetition
- Verify all claims
- Finalize CTAs
