## ARCHITECTURE PRINCIPLES

### Core Rules
- **No thin pages** - Minimum 2,000 words or unique value proposition
- **No orphan pages** - Every page has defined internal linking relationships
- **No duplicate intent** - Each URL targets unique search intent
- **No keyword cannibalization** - Clear keyword mapping per page
- **Defined role per URL** - Authority, Traffic, Conversion, Trust, or Entity building
- **Internal link automation** - Programmatic linking based on page type
- **AI model optimization** - Semantic clarity + entity structure for GEO/AEO
- **Entity graph system** - Knowledge graph, not just entity pages
- **Governance transparency** - Trust through methodology, not just results
- **Proof infrastructure** - Navigable proof system, not just content

### Page Classification System
- **Hub Pages**: Authority builders, link distribution centers
- **Cluster Pages**: High-intent topics, topical authority
- **Service Pages**: Conversion-focused, revenue drivers
- **Programmatic Pages**: Scalable city × service × industry combinations
- **Trust Pages**: Social proof, credibility builders
- **Entity Pages**: Knowledge graph optimization
- **Bridge Pages**: Internal linking connectors
