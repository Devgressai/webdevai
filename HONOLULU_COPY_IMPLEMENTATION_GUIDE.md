**With pricing:**
```json
{
  "priceSpecification": {
    "price": "7500",
    "priceCurrency": "USD",
    "unitText": "MONTH"
  }
}
```

**Why include pricing in schema:**
- **Transparency** - Enterprise buyers expect pricing
- **Rich results** - Can appear in search results
- **Qualification** - Filters out unqualified leads

## Tone & Voice Guidelines

### Honolulu (Enterprise)
- **Confident, not arrogant** - "We're experts" not "We're the best"
- **Data-driven** - Every claim backed by metrics
- **Sophisticated** - Longer sentences, more complex vocabulary
- **ROI-focused** - Emphasize business outcomes
- **Scalable** - Talk about growth, expansion, systems

### Example Comparisons

**Kona (Tourism):**
> "We help Kona businesses dominate local search, attract more tourists, and convert website visitors into customers."

**Honolulu (Enterprise):**
> "We help Honolulu businesses compete at scale. Enterprise-grade technical SEO, AI search optimization, and high-performance web development for medical practices, law firms, real estate agencies, and businesses that demand results."

**Notice:**
- Honolulu is longer and more complex
- Mentions specific industries (medical, legal, real estate)
- Uses "enterprise-grade" and "compete at scale"
- "Demand results" vs. "attract tourists"
