## üîë KEY DIFFERENTIATIONS (Memorize These)

### AI SEO vs Traditional SEO

| Traditional SEO | AI SEO |
|----------------|--------|
| Manual keyword research (weeks) | Automated keyword research (hours) |
| Human analysis of competitors | AI monitors competitors 24/7 |
| Post-publish performance review | Pre-publish performance prediction |
| Limited scalability | High scalability |
| Human-only strategy | AI + human strategy |

**Quick Answer:** AI SEO automates repetitive tasks and provides predictive insights, while traditional SEO relies on manual processes. Both require human expertise for strategy and quality.

---

### AI SEO vs AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)

| AI SEO | AEO |
|--------|-----|
| **HOW** SEO work is done | **WHAT** format content takes |
| Uses AI tools to improve SEO | Structures content as Q&A |
| Methodology/approach | Content format |
| Automates research & analysis | Targets featured snippets |
| Applied across all SEO tasks | Applied to content structure |

**Quick Answer:** AI SEO is about using AI tools within SEO workflows. AEO is about formatting content to answer questions directly. They work together: AI SEO identifies question opportunities, AEO formats content to answer them.

---

### AI SEO vs GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)

| AI SEO | GEO |
|--------|-----|
| Optimizes for Google, Bing search | Optimizes for ChatGPT, Perplexity |
| Targets keyword rankings | Targets AI citations |
| Uses AI for traditional SEO | Optimizes content for AI engines |
| Measures rankings & traffic | Measures citation frequency |
| 25-year-old industry evolved | Emerging practice (2023+) |

**Quick Answer:** AI SEO uses AI tools to optimize for traditional search engines. GEO optimizes content to be cited by AI-generated responses. Both are complementary and should be used together.

## üìä WHEN TO RECOMMEND WHAT

### Recommend AI SEO When:
- Client has competitive market (many competitors)
- Client needs to scale content production
- Client has large website (100+ pages)
- Client wants faster results
- Client has limited in-house SEO resources
- Client values data-driven decisions

### Recommend Traditional SEO When:
- Client has simple website (10-20 pages)
- Client is local-only business with low competition
- Client has very limited budget
- Client prefers hands-on, manual approach
- Client's industry changes slowly

### Recommend AI SEO + AEO When:
- Client's audience asks lots of questions
- Client wants featured snippets
- Client targets voice search
- Client competes for "what is" / "how to" queries
- Client has FAQ-heavy content

### Recommend Full Stack (AI SEO + AEO + GEO) When:
- Client is enterprise-level
- Client faces intense competition
- Client wants maximum visibility
- Client targets tech-savvy audience
- Client has budget for comprehensive strategy
