- **Variation:** Mix branded ("Webvello Hawaii"), topical ("Hawaii digital marketing"), and navigational ("Hawaii") anchors
- **Natural:** Use anchors that fit the sentence naturally
- **Descriptive:** Make anchors descriptive enough to tell users what they'll find
